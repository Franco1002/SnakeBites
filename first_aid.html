First Aid for Snake Bites

What to Do:

  • Stay calm and move away from the snake.
  • Keep the bitten area still and below heart level to reduce venom spread.
  • Remove any tight clothing or jewelry near the bite area.
  • Call emergency services immediately.
  • Wash the bite with soap and water if possible.
  • Apply a clean, dry bandage over the bite.

What NOT to Do:

  • Do NOT apply ice to the bite.
  • Do NOT cut the wound or try to suck out the venom.
  • Do NOT use a tourniquet.
  • Do NOT attempt to catch the snake.
